# GTM Discovery

Use this when Product Intent concerns positioning, launch, pricing, ICP, public product surfaces, buyer conversion, or growth loops.

## Beachhead Segment Filter

Pick a first segment by scoring:

- Burning pain: acute, expensive, frequent, getting worse.
- Willingness to pay: budget and ROI are clear.
- Winnability: you have access, differentiation, and a realistic path to references.
- Referral potential: the segment talks to adjacent high-value segments.

Use a specific segment. "Companies" is too broad. "Series A fintech teams with weekly compliance reporting pain" is closer.

## Cheap Tests

- Message 5 target users with a concrete artifact or offer.
- Create one public page variant and measure click/checkout/contact starts.
- Offer a concierge version before automating.
- Run pricing against current warm prospects before changing checkout architecture.
